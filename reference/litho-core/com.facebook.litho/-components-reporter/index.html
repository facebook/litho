ComponentsReporter

open class ComponentsReporter

This is intended as a hook into android.util.Log, but allows you to provide your own functionality. Use it as

ComponentsReporter.emitMessage(level, message); As a default, it simply calls android.util.Log (see DefaultComponentsReporter). You may supply your own with provide.
